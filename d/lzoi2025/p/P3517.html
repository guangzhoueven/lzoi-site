[POI 2011] WYK-Plot

题目描述

We call any sequence of points in the plane a plot.

We intend to replace a given plot (P1,⋯ ,Pn)(P_1,\cdots,P_n)(P1​,⋯,Pn​) with another that will have at most mmm points (m≤nm\le nm≤n) in such a way that it "resembles" the original plot best.

The new plot is created as follows. The sequence of points P1,⋯ ,PnP_1,\cdots,P_nP1​,⋯,Pn​ can be partitioned into sss (s≤ms\le ms≤m) contiguous subsequences:

$(P_{k_0+1},\cdots,P_{k_1}),(P_{k_1+1},\cdots,P_{k_2}),\cdots,(P_{k_{s-1}+1},\cdots,P_{k_s})$ where 0=k0<k1<k2<⋯<ks=n0=k_0<k_1<k_2<\cdots<k_s=n0=k0​<k1​<k2​<⋯<ks​=n,and afterwards each subsequence (Pki−1+1,⋯ ,Pki)(P_{k_{i-1}+1},\cdots,P_{k_i})(Pki−1​+1​,⋯,Pki​​), for i=1,⋯ ,si=1,\cdots,si=1,⋯,s,is replaced by a new point QiQ_iQi​.

In that case we say that each of the points Pki−1+1,⋯ ,PkiP_{k_{i-1}+1},\cdots,P_{k_i}Pki−1​+1​,⋯,Pki​​ has been contracted to the point QiQ_iQi​.

As a result a new plot, represented by the points Q1,⋯ ,QsQ_1,\cdots,Q_sQ1​,⋯,Qs​, is created.

The measure of such plot's resemblance to the original is the maximum distance of all the points P1,⋯ ,PnP_1,\cdots,P_nP1​,⋯,Pn​ to the point it has been contracted to:

$max_{i=1,\cdots,s}(max_{j=k_{i-1}+1,\cdots,k_i}(d(P_j,Q_i)))$ where d(Pj,Qi)d(P_j,Q_i)d(Pj​,Qi​) denotes the distance between PjP_jPj​ and QiQ_iQi​, given by the well-known formula:

$d((x_1,y_1),(x_2,y_2))=\sqrt{(x_2-x_1)^2+(y_2-y_1)^2}$

An exemplary plot P1,⋯ ,P7P_1,\cdots,P_7P1​,⋯,P7​ and the new plot (Q1,Q2)(Q_1,Q_2)(Q1​,Q2​), where (P1,⋯ ,P4)(P_1,\cdots,P_4)(P1​,⋯,P4​) are contracted to Q1Q_1Q1​, whereas (P5,P6,P7)(P_5,P_6,P_7)(P5​,P6​,P7​) to Q2Q_2Q2​.

For a given plot consisting of nnn points, you are to find the plot that resembles it most while having at most mmm points, where the partitioning into contiguous subsequences is arbitrary.

Due to limited precision of floating point operations, a result is deemed correct if its resemblance to the given plot is larger than the optimum resemblance by at most 0.0000010.0000010.000001.
